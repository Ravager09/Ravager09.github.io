Distribution Analysis

A. Churn Distribution by Subscription Type

  • Implementation: Pie Chart Analysis
  • Purpose: Shows the proportion of churned customers across different subscription categories
  • Methodology:

    • Calculates percentage of churned customers in each segment
    • Visualizes proportions in circular format
  • Insights:

Conclusion

Based on our analysis, we can conclude that:

  • Customer demographics, particularly age, play a significant role in churn behavior.
  • Higher engagement metrics, such as usage frequency and tenure, correlate with lower churn rates.
  • Financial indicators, including payment patterns and total spend, provide valuable insights into churn risk.
  • Service characteristics, like subscription type and contract length, significantly influence customer retention.
  • Timely and positive customer interactions are crucial for maintaining strong relationships and reducing churn.

To reduce churn, we recommend implementing targeted retention strategies for at-risk segments, encouraging upgrades to premium plans, promoting longer contract lengths, and focusing on continuous engagement and value demonstration across the customer lifecycle.
